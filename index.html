              How to play
              
    Rules
    
            
    
            
          
    
          
    
            
    You have 5 shots to guess the mystery football player.
    
            
    Every day a new player sticker is missing from the album. Three clues are revealed from the start:
    
            
      
              
    • World Cup — the tournament they played in
      • 
              
    • Nation — the country they represented
      • 
              
    • Played for — a club from their career
      • 
            
    
            
    Each wrong guess peels back another corner, revealing extra clues in order:
    
            
      
              
    • Miss 1 — Age during that World Cup
      • 
              
    • Miss 2 — Position
      • 
              
    • Miss 3 — Shirt number
      • 
              
    • Miss 4 — Photograph
      • 
            
    
            
    Guess correctly to collect the sticker. A new page opens every day at midnight.
