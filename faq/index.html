FAQ

Where do the player photos come from?

All photos are sourced from Wikipedia (Wikimedia Commons) under Creative Commons licences.

How often does a new player appear?

Every day at midnight, local time. Come back daily to collect your sticker.

Does my progress save if I close the page?

Yes — everything is stored locally on your device. No account or login needed.

Can I play players I missed from previous days?

Yes. Tap Archive to browse and replay any past player. Archive games don't count towards your streak, but the players still appear in your sticker album.

How many editions of ICONED are there?

We continuously update with new editions. Have a suggestion? Let us know!

A player name I typed isn't being accepted — why?

Try without accents or special characters — for example, type Mane instead of Mané.
